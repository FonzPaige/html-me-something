My Favorite things in Life

  • Family
  • Food
  • Music
  • Cars

Family

My family and close group of friends are by far the most important things in life to me. My immediate family includes my Mom, Dad, 2 older sisters, 4 nieces, and 1 nephew. I've yet to have children of my own, but I definitely plan on having a few in the future. My second family includes 6 very close friends whom I consider my brothers, and my beautiful girlfriend Ari. Without this amazing group of people, I couldn't even tell you where I would be in life right now. I live for them, and they live for me.

Food

Food? Don't even get me started on Food! Without food, there is no me. Literally, I'd perish from malnutrition. But on a lighter note, food makes me so damn happy :). My favorite foods are chicken wings, pepperoni pizza, potato chips, pasta, and weirdly enough, asparagus and many other vegetables. I love trying new recipes when I'm cooking, and visiting mom and pop restaraunts when i dine out. I'm all for trying anything once, but if I don't like something, I'll never even think about it again. Though my love for food is insanely high, I still try to eat a balanced diet in order to remain healthy. Food feeds the body, and the soul, so it's imperative to be conscious of what you put in your body.

Music

Music is yet another thing that keeps my spirits high about life. I really appreciate music, and the effort artist make to produce high quality sounds. My favorite genres of music are Hip-Hop and R&B, and a few of my favorite artists in rotation at the moment are Jim Jones, Wiz Khalifa, Jhene Aiko, A Boogie, Nipsey Hussle, Mac Miller, H.e.r, and Melii. My favorite artist of all time is Jay-Z; I know, typical. Though hip-hop and R&B are the main genres I listen to, I make an effort to venture and listen to music outside of the norm. I'm always amazed at the different sounds and vibes that can be produced.

Cars

Cars are what keep me pushing to be successful. I'm really into cars and one day plan to have a stable filled with antique and newer models of all body types, and performances. I currently drive a Camaro, and I'm looking to buy an SUV for my next purchase. Cars are the ultimate status symbols, but even deeper than that I love the way they sound, I love how off-roaders can take you through mountain terrain, and how sports cars can get you from point A to point B rather quickly. In order to fullfill my boyish dreams of owning a garage filled with everthing from lamborghini's, to Chevy Bel-Air's, I have to make smart investments/decisions, and work hard to put myself in a wealthy position. The visualization of me behind the wheel of such cars keep me pushing for everything I want in life.
